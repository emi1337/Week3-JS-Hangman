Welcome to Hang-woman!

First, decide if you want to play a word your friend picked or just guess a computer randomized game by clicking the appropriate button below.

RULES:

  1. You have 6 allowed wrong guesses before you die and lose the game.
  2. If you guess the same letter multiple times, it won't affect your "wrong guesses".
  3. If playing with a friend, look away while they choose your word for you.
